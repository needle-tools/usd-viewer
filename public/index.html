About USD Viewer

A free, in-browser viewer for OpenUSD files (.usd, .usdz, .usda, .usdc), built and maintained by Needle.

It runs OpenUSD compiled to WebAssembly and rendered through the three.js Hydra bridge, so scene composition works right in your browser. Nothing is uploaded; everything runs locally on your device. The viewer is built with Needle Engine, our web 3D engine based on three.js — you can host, optimize and share your own USD files on Needle Cloud.
